Common Questions

What should I expect in my first session?

Your first session is a sacred space for us to connect and understand your unique needs. We'll discuss your history, current challenges, and intentions. I'll explain my approach and we'll begin with gentle practices suited to where you are. There's no pressure—just compassionate presence.

Do you offer virtual sessions?

Yes! All of my coaching, meditation training, and spiritual mentorship services are available virtually via Zoom. Energy healing can also be conducted as distance sessions, which are just as effective as in-person work. This allows me to serve clients worldwide.

What is your cancellation policy?

I understand that life happens. I ask for 24 hours notice for any cancellations or rescheduling. Late cancellations or no-shows may be charged a fee. Emergencies are always handled with compassion—just communicate with me.

I'm new to holistic wellness. Is that okay?

Absolutely! Many of my clients come with no prior experience in meditation, energy work, or holistic practices. I meet you exactly where you are and explain everything in accessible, down-to-earth language. Your curiosity is all you need to begin.
